ABOUT ME

Hi!

I've always been interested in technology. In a previous life I often wrote about things like automation and machine learning as a professional journalist. About a year ago I finally decided to turn that interest into a career in software engineering.

I enrolled in Hack Reactor's full stack software engineering immersive program in September and when I graduated in January I had solid skills in JavaScript, React, and Node, an understanding of both NoSQL and SQL databases, a love of deployment using AWS' services, and experience using those tools in an Agile team environment.

Since graduation I've continued to polish those skills, and learn new ones. In my personal projects I've gained expertise in technologies like TypeScript, React Hooks, GraphQL and SCSS, among others.

I am interested in finding a place to use these skills alongside a supportive and dynamic team.
