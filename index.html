ABOUT ME

Hi!

I've always been interested in technology. In a previous life I often wrote about things like automation and machine learning as a journalist. About two and a half years ago, I finally decided to turn that interest into a career in software engineering.

Since then, I've worked on a number of interesting and challenging projects. I spent nearly a year building React Native apps for different very-early stage startups as I worked freelance alongside three of my fellow graduates from Hack Reactor's software engineering bootcamp.

In December 2010, I began working full-time with Agrian - an agriculture technology company. During my first 8-10 months I worked alongside three other engineers to publish the company's InField app - a record-keeping/messaging app that has already enjoyed wide uptake among large-scale farming operations.

Since Agrian's recent acquisition by Telus Agriculture, my team has stopped adding new features to InField and are now beginning to develop a web/mobile portal that will eventually allow customers access to all the company's products. Like InField, this new project has a heavy focus on visual maps and communication between users, while challenging myself and my team to find solutions for users that move frequently in and out of internet service.

MY WORK

Here are some of the apps I've worked on.


InField

I joined Agrian at the end of 2020 as the fourth engineer on their InField team. As one of two front-end engineers on the team, I touched all parts of the app.

In particular, I put a lot of time into the styling and functionality of the maps, InField's messaging system and hooking the app up to Agrian's custom analytics service.

MostDays

My last project as a freelance engineer was building out the MVP for MostDays - a mental health app that allowed people in addiction recovery to connect with support communities through their phones.

I worked on the app's front-end team alongside three other engineers. We used React Native, React Native Navigation and Redux to build the front-end alongside a UI team feeding us designs and a backend team using PHP and MySQL.
